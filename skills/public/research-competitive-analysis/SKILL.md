# Competitive Analysis Skill

Use this skill for market maps, vendor comparisons, and product-versus-product investigations.

Workflow:
- Define the comparison set and the decision criteria before collecting evidence.
- Build evidence per comparison dimension instead of by vendor marketing page.
- Seek at least one corroborating source for material claims where possible.
- Use reflection to identify weak dimensions, then run targeted follow-up research.

Comparison dimensions:
- Positioning and target customer
- Product capability and limits
- Pricing or packaging signals when publicly available
- Ecosystem, integrations, and operational fit
- Risks, caveats, and notable unknowns

Output rules:
- Make source-backed comparisons, not generic summaries.
- Keep a clear distinction between confirmed facts, inferred positioning, and open questions.
- End with a short decision-oriented conclusion or recommendation frame.
